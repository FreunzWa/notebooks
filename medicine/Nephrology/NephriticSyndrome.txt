====== NephriticSyndrome ======

__Def__
Inflammatory disease of kidney (nephritis) characterised @triad of hypertension, oliguria, haematuria.
__Mx:__
* @nephritis == disruption of BM == thin BM with pores == RBC and proteins can pass into the urine
	* Also %proteinuria == %hydroalbuminaemia
__Sx__:
* Azotemia symptoms (@renal impairment)
* Fluid overload
* %blurred vision
__Types__:
* Younger
	* [[+IgA ]][[+IgA nephropathy|nephropathy]] (most common)
	* Post-Streptococcal Glomerulonephritis
	* Hemolytic Uremic Syndrome
	* Henoch Schonlein purpura
	* Alport syndrome
	* Thin basement membrane nephropathy
	* Membranoproliferative GN
* Adults:
	* Goodpasture syndrome
	* SLE nephritis
	* Infective endocarditis
	* Rapidly progressive GN (crescenteric)
__Ix__:
* UEC (renal impairment, azotemia, hyperkalaemia)
* Dipstick + Urinalysis (RBC microscopy, haematuria, albuminuria)
* Complement levels (reduced @complement mediated causes)
* Kidney biopsy (esp for diagnosis of SLE, crescenteric)
__Rx__:
* Correction of electrolyte derangement
* Treat fluid overload
* Treat hypertension
